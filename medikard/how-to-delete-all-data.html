Medikard - Account & Data Deletion

Medikard stores the following user data:

  • Email address (from Google or Apple Sign-In)
  • User ID (generated for your account)

You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the app:

  • 1. Open Medikard
  • 2. Go to Settings
  • 3. Tap Delete all data
  • 4. Confirm your choice

Account data is deleted immediately upon request and is not retained.
